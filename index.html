The problem

AI makes millions of decisions.
None of them are provable.

01

No explanation

When an AI denies a loan or flags a transaction, neither the company nor the customer can explain why — in a way that satisfies a regulator or stands up in court.

02

No audit trail

AI decisions live in databases that can be edited. There is no tamper-proof record of what was decided, when, by which model, and with what confidence.

03

Regulators are arriving

The EU AI Act, Singapore MAS FEAT, and OJK's new AI guidelines all mandate explainable, auditable AI decisions. The deadline is August 2026. Most companies have nothing.

04

AIDAL solves all three

One API call logs every decision with a cryptographic proof, an AI-generated explanation, and a tamper-evident chain. Regulators can verify everything in seconds.

How it works

One line of code.
Full compliance.

01.

Send the decision

After your AI makes a decision, send it to AIDAL via one API call. Any language, any model, any cloud provider.

02.

We lock it forever

AIDAL cryptographically hashes the decision and chains it to all previous records. It is now mathematically impossible to change without detection.

03.

AI generates the explanation

An LLM automatically writes a plain-English explanation of why the decision was made — compliant with EU AI Act Article 13.

04.

Audit anytime

Regulators hit /verify and get instant proof the ledger is clean. Customers can contest decisions with full transparency. You pass the audit.

# Before AIDAL
outcome = model.predict(applicant)

# After AIDAL — one line added
outcome = model.predict(applicant)

return outcome
